<h2>Gloucester Pre School is a unique learning environment for children 3 to 5 years of age.</h2>
<p>Established in 1972 Gloucester Pre School has a long connected history with our local community.</p>
<p>The classrooms are designed for children to learn in a social cooperative environment following their interests and supporting their development so that they thrive now and into the future.</p>
<p><strong>Operating hours are: 8.15 am - 3.45 pm (school term only)</strong></p>
<p>Pre School is available for children aged 3 to 5 years old.</p>
<p>Working closely with families, we take every opportunity to get to know each child as a unique individual. Our experienced early education professionals plan learning experiences in collaboration with families and children to inspire a love of learning, social engagement and a passion to be curious about the world around them.</p>
<p>Our indoor learning environments provide rich opportunities for learning in areas such as literacy, numeracy, social &amp; emotional development, science, technology &amp; creative arts. The children's day is organised so they can experience long periods of uninterrupted play interspersed with experiences such as music and language.</p>
<p>All children are provided with nutritious meals prepared on the premises in our commercial kitchen each day.</p>
<p>Our young learners live and learn with nature, with sustainable practices embedded into the program. View our Outdoor Learning Environment page for more information.</p>
