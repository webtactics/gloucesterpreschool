<p><strong>Long Day Care (LDC) Enrollment</strong></p>
<p>Gloucester Preschool is able to offer Long Day Care enrollments. Long day Care days are available to children under the age of 6 and operates 8am to 5:15pm, Monday to Friday for 50 weeks per year. The Government's new Child Care Subsidy package will determine the fee per day for LDC enrollments.</p>
<p><strong>Preschool Enrollment</strong></p>
<p>Our Preschool program is available to children 3 years to school age and operates 8:15am - 3:45pm during term time. We are funded by the NSW Government under their Early Childhood Education Program. Two days of free preschool are available for children 3 to 5 years of age. There is a daily charge of $5 to cover the cost of meals. You will not be charged fees for school holidays, but can book into our vacation care program for a small daily fee.</p>
<p><strong>Fee Information</strong></p>
<p>Preschool fees and the Long Day Care fees will be invoiced two weeks in advance as per the Australian Governments New Child Care Package.</p>
<p>Two weeks' notice should be given if your child is leaving Pre-school.</p>
<p>A copy of the fees policy is located in the Parent Login in section under policies</p>
