<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Certificate III in Childhood Education &amp; Care qualified</strong></span></p>
<p>As a CIII you will be responsible for contributing to a high quality early education and care program that supports children's learning and development and is aligned with the Early Years Learning Framework. Specifically, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with your team to develop an educational program that is appropriate to the individual needs and interests of the children</li>
<li>Work collaboratively to ensure the best outcomes for children and families</li>
<li>Assist children to become confident and involved learners</li>
<li>Assess, reflect and share information with parents about the child's progress</li>
<li>Provide children with a safe, welcoming and nurturing environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Essential Criteria CIII Trainee</p>
<p>A current paid working with children check number<br />Agreement to undertake an 18 month traineeship<br />Excellent verbal and written communication skills<br />A friendly and energetic demeanor</p>
<p>The successful candidate/s will be required to successfully complete a National Criminal History Record Check, have a current employee Working with Children Check, sign a Health Declaration, and present evidence of the right to Work in Australia.</p>
<p>Position Terms: Full Time and Part-time position can be negotiated. Shifts between 7.45am and 5.15pm, Monday to Friday. The service closes for 2 weeks over the Christmas period.</p>
<p>Please email your resume to director@gloucesterpreschool.com.au</p>
