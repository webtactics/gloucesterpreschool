<h3>What children learn from the daily program.</h3>
<p>The daily program supports our goals for the children. We want your child to feel secure and independent, to move from one activity to another as easily and confidently as possible.</p>
<p>We also want to provide a variety of learning experiences for a well-rounded education. So we plan a daily program that takes all of these goals into account.</p>
<p>We follow the same routine day after day so children know what will be happening next. The day is a mix of free play, where the children can choose from the activities provided, and formal times where the children sit together and do all the same activities under staff instruction e.g. music, language groups and transition times.</p>

### Lower Content

<h4>Arrival and Departure:</h4>
<p><strong>Pre-School hours are 8:15 am – 3:45 pm</strong><br /> All children must be signed in and out each day using the tablets on the verandah.</p>
<p>Please ensure that a member of staff is aware that your child has arrived in the morning and when he/she is leaving.</p>
<p>Any changes in the arrangements to collect your child should be made aware to the Director either in writing or by telephone. These changes should be written on the sign-in sheet (at the bottom under special instructions).</p>
<p>No child will not be released if we have not been given prior warning. No child may leave the premises with a person other than a parent/guardian or someone appointed on the enrolment form unless the staff has been previously notified. If the person picking up your child is not personally known to the staff on duty that day they will be asked to provide proof of their identity e.g. a driver's license.</p>
